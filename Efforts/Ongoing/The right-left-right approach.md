## Daily practice

Operationalized as a daily triad (skills in `Atlas/Utilities/AI/skills/`,
invokable by slash command or plain words):

- `/morning-descent` — Right = the waking apprehension itself (no tool
  involved); Left = the second brain structuring that reflection against the
  plan ladder and Efforts; Right, returned = a brief appended to the day's
  Now note in `Timeline/Timeline`, after a divider.
- `/session` — the day's bounded working blocks: orient against the brief,
  work one thread from the thread map, bank results to a session ledger.
  Nudges if the morning descent hasn't run yet.
- `/evening-reflect` — the returning arc: audit the day against the brief
  with evidence from the Now notes, percolate events into Timeline, carry or
  close open loops, release.

The name comes from the shape of the day: right-hemisphere apprehension
(whole before parts) structured by a left-hemisphere pass (parts made
explicit), returned to a right-hemisphere reading (the whole, now informed).
See [[McGilchrist - The Divided Brain]] for the underlying distinction.
