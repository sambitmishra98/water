## Purpose

`Timeline/Plans` holds future-facing commitments: the plan ladder from
life-scale intent down through year, month, week, and day, plus dated
reminders.

## The plan ladder

Nested plan notes at decreasing scale (a top life-goal note, then decade /
year / month / week rungs) linked parent to child. The morning-descent skill
walks whatever ladder exists here to locate a waking reflection against
standing intent.

Build the ladder from what real mornings surface; do not backfill it
speculatively. A missing rung is information, not a defect.

## Rules

- Plans state intent; evidence of what actually happened stays in
  `Timeline/Timeline` and `Timeline/Logs`.
- When a plan changes, update it and note the date; the old intent may be
  worth a dated trace rather than silent overwriting.
