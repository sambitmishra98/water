## Purpose

Guide LLM work inside `Atlas`, the stable knowledge layer.

## Inheritance

This file inherits the root agent guide in `[[AGENTS]]`.
It also follows the directory meaning in `[[Atlas/README]]`.
Local rules below add Atlas-specific behavior.

## Allowed actions

- Read `Atlas/README.md` before acting in `Atlas`.
- Suggest clearer names, links, splits, merges, or moves.
- Make narrow edits to stable reference notes when explicitly requested.
- Improve maps, templates, and durable conceptual structure.

## Forbidden actions

- Do not broadly rewrite conceptual notes.
- Do not move unstable notes into or out of `Atlas` without approval.
- Do not convert active work, dated logs, or uncertain research into Atlas notes by default.
- Do not flatten personal nuance into generic summaries.

## Local rules

- Treat `Atlas` as stable knowledge only.
- If a note seems unstable, suggest relocation to `Research/+` or `Efforts/+`.
- Prefer concise conceptual definitions over personal reflection.
- Preserve deliberate links to people, concepts, tools, and source notes.
- Do not remove emotional context from people notes when it is part of the anchor.
- Preserve links from people anchors to relevant `Timeline`, `Efforts`, and `Atlas/Me` notes.
