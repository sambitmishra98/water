## Purpose

`Worlds/Illustrations` is the reusable visual explanation layer.

## Rules

- Prefer editable visual source when possible.
- Mermaid, Excalidraw, LaTeX/TikZ, SVG, generated images, and diagrams may be represented.
- Keep raw or heavy assets outside `Worlds` unless curated.
