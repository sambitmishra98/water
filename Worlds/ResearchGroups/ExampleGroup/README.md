## Purpose

`Worlds/ResearchGroups/ExampleGroup` is a future child vault for group-facing
work (e.g. a lab, a research group, a team you collaborate with).

For now it starts with your own group-facing work, learnings from others,
group-mentioned proposals, project context, coding context, validation
context, paper or proposal seeds, and communication-ready summaries.

## Shareability

This folder may later get its own `.obsidian` and be showcased to the team.

Until then, treat it as user-curated and not automatically team-shareable.
Use a shared drive rather than a code host for sharing and sync, since it may
connect to large data. Heavy material should live in shared storage, a local
`Supplementary/on-drive` if available, or an external archive. The child
vault should store manifests and links, not huge payloads.

## What belongs here

- User-owned group-facing work and learnings.
- Group-mentioned proposals.
- Proposal context and paper seeds.
- Project dashboards and communication summaries.
- Coding, validation, and technical traceability notes.
- Carefully curated context learned from others.

## What does not belong here

- Private parent-vault context unless explicitly curated.
- Raw heavy files or private archives.
- Claims about lab commitments, team positions, or PI preferences without
  evidence.

## Suggested structure

- `Projects`
- `Proposals`
- `Papers`

- `Validation`
