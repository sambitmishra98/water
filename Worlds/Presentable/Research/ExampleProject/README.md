## Purpose

Meeting-ready material for this project: figures, lightweight preview media,
source notes, and exports.

## Rules

- Prefer lightweight previews (e.g. GIFs) for quick Markdown-native previews
  when quality is sufficient.
- Keep source videos, frame sequences, and raw outputs in
  `Supplementary/on-drive` or Drive.
- Curated previews may be linked from presentable notes.
- Pair sources with export paths.
- Do not treat raw daily logs as presentable.
