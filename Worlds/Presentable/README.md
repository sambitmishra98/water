## Purpose

`Worlds/Presentable` stores presentation-ready and meeting-ready material.

This is where conferences, private meeting decks, quick showable notes, slide sources, exports, and presentation assets live.

## Rules

- Prefer editable sources plus export notes.
- Markdown-first is preferred when it supports equations, images, videos, diagrams, and reliable export.
- Hybrid workflows are allowed: PowerPoint, Google Slides, Markdown, Excalidraw, Mermaid, LaTeX/TikZ, PDFs, and images.
- Large assets should be referenced through Drive or Supplementary manifests.
- Keep final exports and source notes clearly linked.
- Prefer lightweight GIFs for quick Markdown-native simulation previews when quality is sufficient.
- Keep source videos, frame sequences, and raw outputs in `Supplementary/on-drive` or Drive.
- Link curated GIFs or preview assets from Presentable notes.
