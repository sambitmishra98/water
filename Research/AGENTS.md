## Purpose

Guide LLM work inside `Research`, the inquiry and incubation layer.

## Inheritance

This file inherits the root agent guide in `[[AGENTS]]`.
It also follows the directory meaning in `[[Research/README]]`.
Local rules below add Research-specific behavior.

## Allowed actions

- Read `Research/README.md` before acting in `Research`.
- Summarize uncertainty, open questions, sources, and candidate next steps.
- Flag notes as `uncertain`, `needs source`, `candidate for Atlas`, or `candidate for Efforts`.
- Suggest links to `Atlas`, `Efforts`, `Timeline`, `Supplementary`, or `Worlds`.

## Forbidden actions

- Do not move notes without approval.
- Do not over-stabilize uncertain notes into confident claims.
- Do not flatten personal, spiritual, sensitive, or intellectual nuance.
- Do not treat exploratory material as publication-ready.
- Do not store or inspect heavy evidence here; link to `Supplementary`.

## Local rules

- Be careful with speculative, personal, spiritual, or sensitive inquiry.
- Preserve uncertainty and source gaps.
- Separate setup, observation, evidence, interpretation, and next diagnostic when reviewing technical or research notes.
- Flag unsupported assumptions, stale conclusions, missing validation, and missing citations.
- Prefer proposed destinations over direct restructuring.
- Distinguish research thinking from active execution.
